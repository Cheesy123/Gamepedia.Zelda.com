Expansion Packs

Gamepedia

Extend your unforgettable adventure in Hyrule with The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild Expansion Pass. The Expansion Pass grants access to three new treasure chests and two DLC packs as they are released. The Expansion Pass is available for both the Nintendo SwitchTM and Wii UTM versions of the game.

DLC Pack 1: Summer 2017

DLC Pack 1 adds The Trial of the Sword, Hard Mode, Hero's Path Mode, travel medallions, and new armor.

DLC Pack 2: Holiday 2017

The second content pack adds a new dungeon, an original story, and even more new challenges.
